Java conventions for date format string

LucidDb will follow the formatting conventions used by java.text.SimpleDateFormat which are as follows:


Field Full Form Short Form
Year yyyy (4 digits) yy (2 digits)
Month MMM (name) MM (2 digits), M (1 or 2 digits)
Day of week EEEE EE
Day of month dd (2 digits) d (1 or 2 digits)
Hour (1-12) hh (2 digits) h (1 or 2 digits)
Hour (0-23) HH (2 digits) H (1 or 2 digits)
Hour (0-11) KK K
Hour (1-24) kk k
Minute mm
Second ss
Millisecond SSS
AM/PM a
Time zone zzzz zz
Day of week in month F (e.g., 3rd Thursday)
Day in year DDD (3 digits D (1, 2, or 3 digits)
Week in year ww
Era (BC/AD) G
